This is my custom statics.lua for the Pointwatch addon in Windower4.

If, like myself, you find that you are tearing your hair out trying to make Pointwatch look nice and show something useful, try deleting your settings.xml in the data folder and then replace your statics.lua with the one found here. You may wish to edit the positional x and y coordinates back to 0, then click and drag to your desired location in-game.

Unload and reload addon in-game. GOOD LUCK

If you want to customize your own changes, look into C-style strings. This is its own syntax that the original developer did not leave any hints about.
